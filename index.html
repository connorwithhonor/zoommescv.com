Real Estate Strategy

Clear guidance for buyers, sellers, and investors—focused on timing, risk, and net results.

Buyer strategy

A clear plan for the next 30 days—based on your timeline, budget, and risk tolerance.

  • How the current buyer process works and what must happen before showings.
  • Costs beyond the down payment—and how to avoid surprise fees.
  • How to compare lender quotes and evaluate true cost of financing.
  • When credits beat repairs (and when repairs are smarter).
  • How to avoid bad deals disguised as “special program” advertising.

Seller strategy

Build a pricing and positioning plan that protects your net and your timeline.

  • How to evaluate market positioning beyond online estimates.
  • What actually moves price: condition, presentation, timing, and buyer psychology.
  • Repairs vs. credits: how to choose the cleanest path to close.
  • Commission conversations: how negotiations are structured today and what options exist.
  • How to reduce chaos and increase control during the transaction.

AI Audits & AI Growth

Evaluate AI risk, data exposure, and governance—then identify high-impact, low-risk growth opportunities.

AI audit (risk + governance)

Surface risk before it becomes costly—privacy, security, compliance, and model reliability.

  • Data handling and exposure review (what goes in, where it goes, and who can access it).
  • Model risk and reliability: hallucinations, bias, and performance drift.
  • Vendor and tool assessment: contracts, retention policies, and security posture.
  • Governance: approvals, logging, monitoring, and human oversight.

AI growth & automation

Pinpoint practical use cases and build a roadmap for measurable ROI.

  • Process mapping to identify the highest-impact automation opportunities.
  • Tool selection and integration planning across your current stack.
  • Human-in-the-loop design to keep quality and accountability high.
  • KPIs and success criteria so wins are measurable and repeatable.
